About Us

Sanctuary of Wind and Fire Church

The Sanctuary of Wind and Fire Assemblies of God, located in Tamale, Ghana, is a vibrant spiritual community dedicated to the worship of God and the transformation of lives through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Founded with a vision to be a "Place of Power, Prayer & Fire," the church has grown into a spiritual haven for many in the region and beyond.

Our Mission

To preach the gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations, making disciples and empowering believers to live a life of purpose and spiritual fire.

Our Vision

To be a leading spiritual center where the presence of God is tangible, and where every individual is equipped to fulfill their divine destiny.

About Pastor Aaron Lambon Fant

Rev. Aaron Lambon Fant is a distinguished Evangelist, Church Planter, and Conference Speaker. He serves as the Lead Pastor of the Sanctuary of Wind and Fire Assemblies of God in Tamale.

Known for his deep spiritual insights and powerful delivery of the word, Pastor Aaron has authored several best-selling books that have touched lives globally. His ministry is characterized by a strong emphasis on prayer, deliverance, and the prophetic.

He is a "still small voice" with a living word for a dying world, committed to the overseas missions project and the expansion of God's kingdom.
